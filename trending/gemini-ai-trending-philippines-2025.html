What is Gemini AI?

Gemini AI is Google's latest multimodal AI model, capable of processing text, images, audio, and video simultaneously. Launched in late 2024, it powers various Google products, from search enhancements to creative tools. Unlike predecessors, Gemini excels in complex reasoning, coding, and real-time collaboration, making it a versatile assistant for personal and professional use.

Key Features Driving Adoption

Gemini's multimodal capabilities allow users to analyze images (e.g., translating signs) or generate content from prompts. Integration with Google Workspace boosts productivity, while safety features ensure ethical use. In the Philippines, it's popular for tutoring, recipe generation, and travel planning.

Public Reactions and User Experiences
