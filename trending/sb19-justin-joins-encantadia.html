SB19's Justin de Dios Joins Encantadia: A New Chapter in Philippine Entertainment

By John Paul Pajaro on 2025-09-20

In a move that's set the Philippine entertainment scene ablaze, SB19's Justin de Dios has been announced as part of the cast for the highly anticipated revival of the iconic fantasy series Encantadia. As of September 20, 2025, hashtags like #SB19_JUSTIN, #EncantaJAH, and #AvisalaAktorJustin are dominating Twitter trends in the Philippines, with fans expressing overwhelming excitement for this crossover between P-pop royalty and one of the country's most beloved TV franchises. This blog post explores the announcement, Justin's background, Encantadia's legacy, fan reactions, and the broader implications for Philippine media. With SB19 continuing to break barriers, Justin's acting debut could mark a new era where idols seamlessly transition into diverse entertainment roles.

Who is Justin de Dios? A Quick Look at SB19's Visual and Creative Force

For those unfamiliar with the P-pop sensation, Justin de Dios is the main visual and one of the creative minds behind SB19, the first Filipino group to chart on Billboard's Hot Trending Songs. Born on July 7, 1998, Justin—often called "Jah" by fans—joined SB19 in 2018 after rigorous training under ShowBT Entertainment. Known for his striking looks, smooth vocals, and choreography skills, he's also a talented director and producer, having helmed music videos and concepts for the group. SB19's hits like "Gento" and "Mapa" have propelled them to international fame, with sold-out tours and collaborations that put Philippine music on the global map. Justin's entry into acting isn't entirely surprising; he's expressed interest in storytelling through various mediums, and this role in Encantadia feels like a natural extension of his artistic versatility.

Encantadia's Enduring Legacy in Philippine Television

Encantadia, created by GMA Network, first aired in 2005 and became a cultural phenomenon, blending fantasy, drama, and Philippine folklore. The series follows the Sang'gres—guardians of magical gems—in a world of enchantment, betrayal, and heroism. Its 2016 reboot, Encantadia: Sang'gre, kept the magic alive with stunning visuals and star-studded casts. Now, in 2025, the franchise is reviving with new storylines, and Justin's casting as a "kakampi" (ally) from Devas has fans buzzing. The teaser, released on September 19, 2025, shows Justin in ethereal attire, greeting with "Avisala," the show's signature salutation. This addition promises to infuse fresh energy into the series, appealing to younger audiences who grew up with both Encantadia and SB19.

The Announcement: How It Broke the Internet

The news dropped via Encantadia's official social media on September 19, 2025, with a teaser video featuring Justin de Dios stepping into the mythical realm. "Isang kakampi na isinugo ng Devas! Avisala, Justin de Dios! Maligayang pagdating sa Encantadia!" the post read, sending A'TIN (SB19 fans) into a frenzy. Within hours, #SB19_JUSTIN climbed to #1 on Philippine Twitter trends, with related tags like #EncantaJAH and #AvisalaAktorJustin following suit. Fan accounts flooded timelines with edits, memes, and celebratory posts, while SB19 members like Pablo and Josh shared supportive messages. The timing aligns with SB19's ongoing world tour, showcasing Justin's ability to juggle music and acting careers seamlessly.

Fan Reactions: From Excitement to Creative Outpourings

A'TIN and Encantadiks (Encantadia fans) have united in celebration, with Twitter abuzz over Justin's "medyo-bad-boy" look in the teaser. One fan tweeted, "From creating dreams behind the scenes to finally stepping into the spotlight—our dreamsmith turned into the dream!" Collaborative fanbases like Guardians of Jah posted edits blending SB19 aesthetics with Encantadia lore. Reactions highlight pride in Justin's growth, with comments like "Direk Jah to Actor Jah—proud of you!" The trend's international reach, trending in countries like Thailand and Vietnam, underscores SB19's global fanbase. Some fans speculate on his role, imagining him as a powerful ally or even a love interest for a Sang'gre.

Impact on P-pop and Philippine Television

This casting is more than a celebrity cameo; it's a milestone for P-pop's integration into mainstream media. SB19 has already shattered ceilings as the first Southeast Asian act nominated for Billboard Music Awards, and Justin's role could open doors for more idols in acting. For Encantadia, it attracts a younger demographic, blending nostalgia with fresh talent. In a post-pandemic era where mental health and escapism are key, shows like this provide comfort, while SB19's positive messaging aligns with themes of resilience. Economically, it boosts tourism and merchandise, with fans eager for Encantadia-themed SB19 collabs.
